Yatsura ga Ultimate Force Zero! (2012)
Yatsura ga Ultimate Force Zero! (奴らがウルティメイトフォースゼロ！, Yatsura ga Urutimeito Fōsu Zero!, lit. They are the Ultimate Force Zero!) is the insert theme for the Ultimate Force Zero. It was notably used as the ending theme for Ultra Zero Fight.

Artist: voyager
Released: 2012
Album: Ultra Zero Fight original Soundtrack
